Platform Overview
Why Sahrana?

Algeria has 2 million km² of Saharan territory with untapped agricultural potential. Sahrana combines machine learning with agronomic science to help farmers make data-driven decisions in one of the world's most challenging environments.

🌱

Crop Intelligence

AI model trained on 15 desert-adapted crops, analyzing soil, climate, and nutrient data to find the perfect match for your land.

💧

Smart Irrigation

Precise irrigation schedules that minimize water waste while maximizing yield — critical in water-scarce arid regions.

☀️

Solar Optimization

Leverage Algeria's exceptional solar resources with daily energy availability forecasts to power irrigation pumps sustainably.

Classification

The Sahrana model uses a Gradient Boosting Classifier trained on 5,000+ synthetic samples representing Algerian desert agro-climatic conditions. It analyzes 10 environmental features to predict the optimal crop among 15 desert-adapted varieties.
