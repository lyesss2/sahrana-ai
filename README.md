# sahrana-ai

**Project Description**

*Sahrana* is an AI-powered agritech platform designed to support sustainable agriculture in Algeria’s arid regions. The system analyzes environmental and land data such as soil type, temperature, humidity, water availability, and solar exposure to identify the most suitable crops for a specific desert area. By combining agricultural intelligence with energy optimization, the platform helps farmers, agribusinesses, and decision-makers make informed choices about crop selection, irrigation timing, and resource management. The goal is to unlock the agricultural potential of the Sahara while reducing water waste, improving solar energy usage, and contributing to long-term food security.

For this project, we will develop an **AI model** and a **responsive web dashboard**. The AI model will analyze environmental inputs and generate predictions including recommended crops, expected yield, irrigation scheduling, and solar energy availability. The responsive web dashboard will provide an intuitive interface where users can enter land and climate data, visualize AI predictions, and monitor key insights such as crop recommendations, irrigation timing, and resource efficiency metrics. The platform will be designed to work across desktop and mobile devices, enabling both farmers and agricultural stakeholders to easily access and use the system.
